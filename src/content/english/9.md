持而盈之，

Persisting in fulfilling it,

不如其已。

Is inferior to stopping in time.

揣而锐之，

Protecting and sharpening it,

不可长保。

Can not be preserved forever.

金玉满堂，

Treasure filled halls,

莫之能守。

Can not be defended.

富贵而骄，

Wealth and pride,

自遗其咎。

Lead to self-loss and bad fortune.

功成身退，

Accomplish and retire,

天之道。

The universal way.
